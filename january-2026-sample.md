# TODDLER ADAPTATIONS

Most meals can be made milder for your 3-year-old:

- **Curries/spicy dishes:** Set aside portion before adding chilli
- **Stir-fries:** Reduce soy sauce, no chilli
- **Bolognese:** Standard recipe is already toddler-friendly
- **Fish:** Remove bones, cut into small pieces
- **Roasts:** Small portions, cut meat small, extra gravy

**Toddler-approved favourites:**
- Fish fingers + chips
- Pasta with mild sauce
- Bangers & mash
- Chicken strips
- Rice dishes
