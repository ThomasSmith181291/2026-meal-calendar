Mushroom Lasagne with Prosciutto

Pork
Prep: 20 mins
Cook: 40 mins
Serves: 4

Ingredients

  • mushrooms 250 g
  • lasagne sheets 250 g
  • prosciutto 4 tbsp
  • creme fraiche 4 tbsp
  • parmesan 4 tbsp

Method

  1. Saute mushrooms until moisture released 2
  2. Make white sauce with milk and flour 3
  3. Add prosciutto to sauce 4
  4. Layer lasagne sheets, mushroom sauce and cream 5
  5. Top with cheese and egg mixture 6
  6. Bake until golden
