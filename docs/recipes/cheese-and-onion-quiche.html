Cheese and Onion Quiche

Vegetarian
Prep: 20 mins
Cook: 50 mins
Serves: 6

Ingredients

  • shortcrust pastry 320 g
  • onion 3
  • mature cheddar 150 g
  • egg 3
  • double cream 200 ml

Method

  1. Line tart tin with pastry, blind bake 2
  2. Caramelise onions slowly 3
  3. Beat eggs with cream 4
  4. Add cheese and onions to pastry case 5
  5. Pour over egg mixture 6
  6. Bake until golden and set
