Weekend Breakfast

French Toast

15 mins | Serves 2
Ingredients:
  • 4 thick slices bread (brioche is best)
  • 2 eggs
  • 100ml milk
  • 1 tsp cinnamon
  • 1 tsp vanilla
  • Butter for frying
  • Berries, maple syrup to serve
Method:

Whisk eggs, milk, cinnamon, vanilla. Dip bread slices in mixture. Fry in butter 2-3 mins each side until golden. Serve with berries and maple syrup.

Lunches

Mon

Carrot & coriander soup

30 mins | Serves 4
Ingredients:
  • 500g carrots, chopped
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1L veg stock
  • 1 tsp ground coriander
  • Fresh coriander
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
Method:

Soften onion in oil 5 mins. Add carrots, ground coriander, stock. Simmer 20 mins until carrots soft. Blend smooth, serve with fresh coriander.

5 mins | Serves 1
Ingredients:

  • 75g cheddar, grated
  • Butter
Method:

Butter bread on outside. Fill with cheese. Fry in pan until golden both sides and cheese melted.

Prawn mayo wrap

5 mins | Serves 1
Ingredients:
  • 100g cooked prawns
  • 2 tbsp mayo
  • Squeeze lemon juice
  • 1 wrap
  • Lettuce, cucumber
Method:

Mix prawns with mayo and lemon. Lay wrap flat, add lettuce, cucumber, prawn mixture. Roll up tightly.

Thu

Cheese omelette

10 mins | Serves 1
Ingredients:
  • 3 eggs
  • 50g cheese, grated
  • 1 tbsp butter
  • Salt & pepper
Method:

Beat eggs, season. Melt butter in pan over medium heat. Pour in eggs, swirl to cover base. When nearly set, add cheese to one half, fold over. Serve immediately.

Fri

Tuna melt

10 mins | Serves 1
Ingredients:
  • 1 tin tuna, drained
  • 2 tbsp mayo
  • 2 slices bread
  • 50g cheddar, sliced
Method:

Mix tuna with mayo. Toast bread lightly, spread tuna mixture on top, add cheese. Grill until cheese melted and bubbling.

Dinners

Mon

Cottage Pie

1 hour | Serves 4
Ingredients:
  • 500g beef mince
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 carrots, diced
  • 400ml beef stock
  • 2 tbsp tomato puree
  • 1 tsp Worcestershire sauce
  • 800g potatoes
  • 50g butter
  • 50ml milk
Method:

Brown mince, add onion, carrots. Add stock, puree, Worcestershire, simmer 20 mins. Boil potatoes, mash with butter and milk. Put mince in dish, top with mash. Bake 200C 25 mins until golden.

Tue

Fish Tacos

25 mins | Serves 4
Ingredients:
  • 400g white fish fillets
  • 2 tsp cumin
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 8 small tortillas
  • 1/4 cabbage, shredded
  • Lime
  • Sour cream
  • Fresh coriander
Method:

Season fish with cumin, paprika, salt. Pan fry or grill 3-4 mins each side. Flake fish. Warm tortillas. Fill with cabbage, fish, sour cream, coriander, lime juice.

Wed

Mushroom Risotto

35 mins | Serves 4
Ingredients:
  • 300g risotto rice
  • 300g mixed mushrooms, sliced
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 150ml white wine
  • 1L hot veg stock
  • 50g parmesan
  • 2 tbsp butter
Method:

Fry mushrooms in butter, set aside. Cook onion and garlic, add rice, stir 1 min. Add wine, stir until absorbed. Add stock ladle by ladle, stirring, until rice is creamy (20 mins). Stir in mushrooms, parmesan, butter.

Thu

Chicken Korma

35 mins | Serves 4
Ingredients:
  • 500g chicken breast, cubed
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 3 tbsp korma paste
  • 200ml coconut cream
  • 100g ground almonds
  • 50g sultanas
  • Fresh coriander
  • Rice to serve
Method:

Fry chicken until golden, set aside. Cook onion 5 mins, add korma paste, cook 1 min. Add coconut cream, almonds, sultanas. Return chicken, simmer 15 mins. Serve with rice and coriander.

Fri

Lasagne

1 hour 15 mins | Serves 6
Ingredients:
  • 500g beef mince
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 tins chopped tomatoes
  • Lasagne sheets
  • 50g butter
  • 50g flour
  • 500ml milk
  • 100g parmesan
  • 100g mozzarella
Method:

Make meat sauce: brown mince, add onion, tomatoes, simmer 20 mins. Make white sauce: melt butter, add flour, gradually add milk, stir until thick. Layer: meat, pasta, white sauce. Repeat. Top with cheese. Bake 180C 40 mins.

Sat

Lamb Koftas

25 mins | Serves 4
Ingredients:
  • 500g lamb mince
  • 1 onion, grated
  • 2 garlic cloves, crushed
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 1 tsp coriander
  • Fresh mint
  • Pitta bread
  • Tzatziki, salad to serve
Method:

Mix lamb, onion, garlic, spices, mint. Shape into sausages around skewers. Grill or BBQ 10-12 mins turning. Serve in pitta with tzatziki and salad.

Sun

Sausage Casserole

45 mins | Serves 4
Ingredients:
  • 8 pork sausages
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 2 peppers, sliced
  • 2 tins chopped tomatoes
  • 1 tin cannellini beans
  • 2 tsp smoked paprika
  • Crusty bread to serve
Method:

Brown sausages in pan, set aside. Cook onion and peppers 5 mins. Add tomatoes, paprika, beans, return sausages. Cover, simmer 30 mins. Serve with crusty bread.
