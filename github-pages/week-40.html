Weekend Breakfast

Shakshuka

25 mins | Serves 2
Ingredients:
  • 1 tin chopped tomatoes
  • 1 red pepper, diced
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 4 eggs
  • Fresh coriander
  • Crusty bread
Method:

Fry onion and pepper 5 mins. Add garlic, spices, cook 1 min. Add tomatoes, simmer 10 mins. Make 4 wells, crack in eggs. Cover, cook 5-8 mins until eggs set. Serve with coriander and bread.

Lunches

Mon

Tomato soup

25 mins | Serves 4
Ingredients:
  • 2 tins chopped tomatoes
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 500ml veg stock
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • Fresh basil
Method:

Soften onion and garlic in oil. Add tomatoes, stock, sugar. Simmer 15 mins. Blend smooth, season, serve with basil and crusty bread.

Tue

Egg fried rice

15 mins | Serves 2
Ingredients:
  • 300g cooked rice (cold)
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • 100g peas
  • 2 spring onions, sliced
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 2 tbsp oil
Method:

Heat oil in wok, scramble eggs, set aside. Stir-fry rice 2 mins, add peas, cook 2 mins. Add eggs, spring onions, soy sauce. Toss together.

Dinners

Mon

Chilli Con Carne

45 mins | Serves 4
Ingredients:
  • 500g beef mince
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 1 tin chopped tomatoes
  • 1 tin kidney beans
  • 2 tsp cumin
  • 1 tsp chilli powder
  • Rice, sour cream to serve
Method:

Brown mince, add onion, garlic. Add spices, cook 1 min. Add tomatoes, beans. Simmer 30 mins. Serve with rice, sour cream, grated cheese.

Tue

Chicken Leek Pie

1 hour | Serves 4
Ingredients:
  • 500g chicken breast, cubed
  • 3 leeks, sliced
  • 200ml chicken stock
  • 200ml double cream
  • 1 tbsp Dijon mustard
  • 1 sheet puff pastry
  • 1 egg, beaten
Method:

Poach chicken in stock 15 mins, remove. Cook leeks in stock until soft. Add cream, mustard, return chicken. Pour into pie dish. Top with pastry, brush with egg. Bake 200C 25 mins until golden.

Wed

Mushroom Risotto

35 mins | Serves 4
Ingredients:
  • 300g risotto rice
  • 300g mixed mushrooms, sliced
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 150ml white wine
  • 1L hot veg stock
  • 50g parmesan
  • 2 tbsp butter
Method:

Fry mushrooms in butter, set aside. Cook onion and garlic, add rice, stir 1 min. Add wine, stir until absorbed. Add stock ladle by ladle, stirring, until rice is creamy (20 mins). Stir in mushrooms, parmesan, butter.

Thu

Beef Stroganoff

25 mins | Serves 4
Ingredients:
  • 500g beef sirloin, sliced thin
  • 250g mushrooms, sliced
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 200ml beef stock
  • 150ml sour cream
  • 1 tbsp paprika
  • Rice or pasta to serve
Method:

Fry beef quickly over high heat, set aside. Cook onion, garlic, mushrooms 5 mins. Add paprika, stock, simmer 5 mins. Return beef, stir in sour cream. Serve with rice or pasta.

Fish Tacos

25 mins | Serves 4
Ingredients:
  • 400g white fish fillets
  • 2 tsp cumin
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 8 small tortillas
  • 1/4 cabbage, shredded
  • Lime
  • Sour cream
  • Fresh coriander
Method:

Season fish with cumin, paprika, salt. Pan fry or grill 3-4 mins each side. Flake fish. Warm tortillas. Fill with cabbage, fish, sour cream, coriander, lime juice.

Lamb Koftas

25 mins | Serves 4
Ingredients:
  • 500g lamb mince
  • 1 onion, grated
  • 2 garlic cloves, crushed
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 1 tsp coriander
  • Fresh mint
  • Pitta bread
  • Tzatziki, salad to serve
Method:

Mix lamb, onion, garlic, spices, mint. Shape into sausages around skewers. Grill or BBQ 10-12 mins turning. Serve in pitta with tzatziki and salad.

Sun

Chicken Tikka Masala

40 mins | Serves 4
Ingredients:
  • 500g chicken breast, cubed
  • 2 tbsp tikka paste
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 1 tin chopped tomatoes
  • 200ml double cream
  • Fresh coriander
  • Rice to serve
Method:

Coat chicken in half the tikka paste. Fry chicken until golden, set aside. Cook onion and garlic, add remaining paste, tomatoes. Simmer 10 mins, add cream and chicken. Cook 10 mins. Serve with rice and coriander.
