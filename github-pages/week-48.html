Week 48

23 Nov - 29 Nov 2026

Weekend Breakfast

Eggs Florentine

20 mins | Serves 2
Ingredients:
  • 4 eggs
  • 200g spinach
  • 2 English muffins
  • Hollandaise sauce
  • Butter
Method:

Wilt spinach in butter. Toast muffins. Poach eggs. Layer muffin, spinach, egg, hollandaise.

Lunches

Mon

Cheese on toast

5 mins | Serves 1
Ingredients:
  • 2 slices bread
  • 75g cheddar, grated
  • Worcestershire sauce
Method:

Toast bread one side. Flip, cover with cheese, dash of Worcestershire. Grill until bubbling.

Tue

Sardines on toast

5 mins | Serves 1
Ingredients:
  • 1 tin sardines
  • 2 slices bread
  • Butter
  • Lemon
  • Black pepper
Method:

Toast and butter bread. Top with sardines, lemon juice, pepper.

Wed

Egg fried rice

15 mins | Serves 2
Ingredients:
  • 300g cooked rice (cold)
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • 100g peas
  • 2 spring onions
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
Method:

Scramble eggs, set aside. Stir-fry rice, add peas. Add eggs, spring onions, soy sauce.

Thu

Quesadilla

10 mins | Serves 1
Ingredients:
  • 2 tortillas
  • 75g cheddar, grated
  • Sliced peppers
  • Salsa, sour cream
Method:

Layer cheese and peppers between tortillas. Dry fry until golden both sides. Serve with salsa and sour cream.

Fri

Avocado on toast

5 mins | Serves 1
Ingredients:
  • 1 avocado
  • 2 slices sourdough
  • Lemon juice
  • Chilli flakes
  • Salt
Method:

Toast bread. Mash avocado with lemon, salt. Spread on toast, sprinkle chilli flakes.

Sat

Greek salad

10 mins | Serves 2
Ingredients:
  • 1 cucumber, chunked
  • 4 tomatoes, chunked
  • 1 red onion, sliced
  • 100g feta
  • Olives
  • Olive oil
  • Oregano
Method:

Combine vegetables. Add feta and olives. Dress with olive oil, oregano.

Sun

Caesar salad

15 mins | Serves 2
Ingredients:
  • 1 romaine lettuce
  • 1 chicken breast, cooked and sliced
  • Croutons
  • Parmesan
  • Caesar dressing
Method:

Tear lettuce. Top with chicken, croutons, parmesan. Drizzle with dressing.

Dinners

Mon

Beef Fajitas

25 mins | Serves 4
Ingredients:
  • 500g beef steak, sliced
  • 2 peppers, sliced
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 2 tbsp fajita seasoning
  • 8 tortillas
  • Sour cream, guacamole
Method:

Toss beef in seasoning. Fry beef 3 mins. Add peppers, onion, cook 5 mins. Serve in warm tortillas.

Tue

Tuna Pasta Bake

35 mins | Serves 4
Ingredients:
  • 300g pasta
  • 2 tins tuna
  • 1 tin sweetcorn
  • 50g butter
  • 50g flour
  • 500ml milk
  • 150g cheddar
Method:

Cook pasta. Make cheese sauce with butter, flour, milk, half cheese. Mix pasta, tuna, sweetcorn, sauce. Top with cheese. Bake 200C 20 mins.

Wed

Vegetable Fajitas

25 mins | Serves 4
Ingredients:
  • 2 peppers, sliced
  • 1 courgette, sliced
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 1 tin black beans
  • 2 tbsp fajita seasoning
  • 8 tortillas
  • Sour cream, salsa
Method:

Fry vegetables with seasoning 10 mins. Add beans, heat through. Serve in tortillas with sour cream and salsa.

Thu

Lasagne

1 hr 15 mins | Serves 6
Ingredients:
  • 500g beef mince
  • 1 onion
  • 2 tins tomatoes
  • Lasagne sheets
  • 50g butter
  • 50g flour
  • 500ml milk
  • 100g parmesan
Method:

Make meat sauce: brown mince, add onion, tomatoes, simmer 20 mins. Make white sauce: butter, flour, milk. Layer meat, pasta, white sauce. Top with cheese. Bake 180C 40 mins.

Fri

Smoked Haddock Risotto

35 mins | Serves 4
Ingredients:
  • 400g smoked haddock
  • 300g risotto rice
  • 1 onion
  • 150ml white wine
  • 1L fish stock
  • 100g peas
  • 50g parmesan
Method:

Poach haddock, flake. Cook onion, add rice. Add wine, then stock ladle by ladle. Add peas and haddock last 5 mins. Stir in parmesan.

Sat

Meatballs in Tomato Sauce

40 mins | Serves 4
Ingredients:
  • 500g beef mince
  • 1 onion, grated
  • 50g breadcrumbs
  • 1 egg
  • 2 tins tomatoes
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • Fresh basil
  • Spaghetti
Method:

Mix mince, onion, breadcrumbs, egg. Roll into balls. Make sauce with tomatoes, garlic. Add meatballs, simmer 25 mins. Serve with spaghetti.

Sun

Cauliflower Cheese

35 mins | Serves 4
Ingredients:
  • 1 large cauliflower
  • 50g butter
  • 50g flour
  • 500ml milk
  • 200g cheddar
  • 1 tsp mustard
Method:

Boil cauliflower 5 mins. Make sauce with butter, flour, milk, most of cheese and mustard. Put cauliflower in dish, pour over sauce, top with cheese. Bake 200C 20 mins.
