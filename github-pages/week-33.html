Weekend Breakfast

Avocado Toast with Eggs

10 mins | Serves 2
Ingredients:
  • 2 ripe avocados
  • 4 slices sourdough
  • 4 eggs
  • Chilli flakes
  • Lemon juice
  • Salt & pepper
Method:

Toast bread. Mash avocados with lemon, salt, pepper. Poach eggs. Spread avocado on toast, top with poached eggs, sprinkle chilli flakes.

Lunches

Mon

Ham & cheese toastie

5 mins | Serves 1
Ingredients:
  • 2 slices bread
  • 2 slices ham
  • 50g cheddar cheese, grated
  • Butter
Method:

Butter bread on outside. Layer ham and cheese inside. Fry in pan or toast in sandwich maker until golden and cheese melted.

Tue

Leek & potato soup

30 mins | Serves 4
Ingredients:
  • 2 leeks, sliced
  • 3 potatoes, diced
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1L chicken stock
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 100ml cream
  • Salt & pepper
Method:

Melt butter, soften leeks and onion 5 mins. Add potatoes and stock, simmer 20 mins until soft. Blend until smooth, stir in cream, season.

Wed

Tomato soup

25 mins | Serves 4
Ingredients:
  • 2 tins chopped tomatoes
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 500ml veg stock
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • Fresh basil
Method:

Soften onion and garlic in oil. Add tomatoes, stock, sugar. Simmer 15 mins. Blend smooth, season, serve with basil and crusty bread.

BLT sandwich

10 mins | Serves 1
Ingredients:
  • 3 rashers bacon
  • 2 slices bread, toasted
  • Lettuce leaves
  • 1 tomato, sliced
  • 2 tbsp mayonnaise
Method:

Fry bacon until crispy. Toast bread, spread with mayo. Layer lettuce, tomato, bacon. Season with pepper.

Fri

Welsh rarebit

15 mins | Serves 2
Ingredients:
  • 200g mature cheddar, grated
  • 25g butter
  • 1 tbsp flour
  • 100ml beer or milk
  • 1 tsp mustard
  • 4 slices bread
Method:

Melt butter, stir in flour. Add beer/milk, stir until thick. Add cheese and mustard, stir until melted. Toast bread, spread mixture on top, grill until bubbling.

Egg mayo sandwich

15 mins | Serves 2
Ingredients:
  • 4 eggs, hard boiled
  • 3 tbsp mayonnaise
  • 1 tsp mustard
  • Salt & pepper
  • Bread

Boil eggs 10 mins, cool, peel and mash. Mix with mayo, mustard, season well. Spread on bread with cress.

Dinners

Mon

Shepherd's Pie

1 hour | Serves 4
Ingredients:
  • 500g lamb mince
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 carrots, diced
  • 400ml lamb stock
  • 2 tbsp tomato puree
  • 1 tsp rosemary
  • 800g potatoes
  • 50g butter
  • 50ml milk
Method:

Brown lamb mince, add onion, carrots. Add stock, puree, rosemary, simmer 20 mins. Boil potatoes, mash with butter and milk. Put lamb in dish, top with mash. Bake 200C 25 mins until golden.

Tue

Chicken Katsu Curry

35 mins | Serves 4
Ingredients:
  • 4 chicken breasts
  • 100g flour
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • 150g panko breadcrumbs
  • For sauce: 1 onion, 2 tbsp curry powder, 1 tbsp flour, 400ml chicken stock, 1 tbsp honey
  • Rice to serve
Method:

Make sauce: fry onion, add curry powder and flour, add stock, simmer 10 mins, blend. Coat chicken in flour, egg, panko. Shallow fry 5 mins each side. Slice, serve with rice and sauce.

Wed

Cauliflower Cheese

35 mins | Serves 4
Ingredients:
  • 1 large cauliflower
  • 50g butter
  • 50g flour
  • 500ml milk
  • 200g mature cheddar, grated
  • 1 tsp mustard
Method:

Break cauliflower into florets, boil 5 mins until just tender. Make sauce: melt butter, add flour, gradually add milk stirring. Add most of cheese and mustard. Put cauliflower in dish, pour over sauce, top with remaining cheese. Bake 200C 20 mins.

Thu

Chicken Fajitas

25 mins | Serves 4
Ingredients:
  • 500g chicken breast, sliced
  • 2 peppers, sliced
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 2 tbsp fajita seasoning
  • 8 tortillas
  • Sour cream, guacamole, salsa to serve
Method:

Toss chicken in seasoning. Fry chicken 5 mins until cooked. Add peppers and onion, cook 5 mins. Warm tortillas. Serve chicken in tortillas with toppings.

Fri

Fish & Chips

45 mins | Serves 4
Ingredients:
  • 4 cod fillets
  • 150g plain flour
  • 200ml cold sparkling water
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1kg potatoes, cut into chips
  • Oil for frying
  • Mushy peas, tartare sauce
Method:

Soak chips in water 30 mins, dry well. Fry chips at 160C 5 mins, then 190C until golden. Mix flour, baking powder, water for batter. Dip fish in batter, fry at 180C 6-8 mins. Serve with chips, peas, tartare.

Sat

Beef Stew

2 hours | Serves 4
Ingredients:
  • 500g stewing beef, cubed
  • 2 onions, quartered
  • 4 carrots, chunked
  • 4 potatoes, chunked
  • 500ml beef stock
  • 2 tbsp flour
  • 2 tbsp tomato puree
  • Fresh thyme
Method:

Toss beef in flour, brown in batches. Add onions, cook 3 mins. Add all ingredients to pot, bring to simmer. Cover, cook gently 1.5-2 hours until beef is tender. Season well.

Sunday Roast (for 7)

Roast Beef

1.5 hours + resting | Serves 6-8
Ingredients:
  • 1.5kg beef joint (rib or sirloin)
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt & pepper
  • Fresh rosemary & thyme
Method:

Remove beef from fridge 1 hour before. Preheat oven to 220C. Rub with oil, season generously. Roast 20 mins, reduce to 170C. Cook 15 mins per 500g for medium-rare. Rest 30 mins before carving.

Sides

Roast Potatoes

1 hour | Serves 6
Ingredients:
  • 1.5kg potatoes, peeled and quartered
  • 4 tbsp goose fat or olive oil
  • Salt
  • Fresh rosemary (optional)
Method:

Boil potatoes 10 mins, drain. Shake in pan to roughen edges. Heat fat in roasting tin at 200C. Add potatoes, turn to coat. Roast 45-50 mins, turning halfway, until golden and crispy.

Yorkshire Puddings

30 mins | Serves 12
Ingredients:
  • 140g plain flour
  • 4 eggs
  • 200ml milk
  • Sunflower oil
Method:

Mix flour, eggs, milk until smooth. Rest 30 mins. Put 1 tsp oil in each hole of 12-hole muffin tin. Heat at 230C until smoking. Quickly pour in batter. Bake 20-25 mins until risen and golden. Don't open oven door!

Onion Gravy

25 mins | Serves 6
Ingredients:
  • 2 onions, sliced
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1 tbsp flour
  • 500ml beef stock
  • Splash of red wine (optional)
  • Meat juices from roasting tin
Method:

Cook onions in butter over low heat 15 mins until soft and golden. Add flour, stir 1 min. Add wine if using, then stock and meat juices. Simmer 5 mins, season.
