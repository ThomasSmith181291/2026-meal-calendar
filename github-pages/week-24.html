Week 24

08 Jun - 14 Jun 2026

Weekend Breakfast

Avocado on Toast

10 mins | Serves 2
Ingredients:
  • 2 avocados
  • 4 slices sourdough
  • 4 eggs
  • Chilli flakes
  • Lemon
  • Salt & pepper
Method:

Toast bread. Mash avocados with lemon, seasoning. Poach eggs. Spread avocado on toast, top with egg, chilli flakes.

Lunches

Mon

Cheese on toast

5 mins | Serves 1
Ingredients:
  • 2 slices bread
  • 75g cheddar, grated
  • Worcestershire sauce
Method:

Toast bread one side. Flip, cover with cheese, dash of Worcestershire. Grill until bubbling.

Tue

Sardines on toast

5 mins | Serves 1
Ingredients:
  • 1 tin sardines
  • 2 slices bread
  • Butter
  • Lemon
  • Black pepper
Method:

Toast and butter bread. Top with sardines, lemon juice, pepper.

Wed

Egg fried rice

15 mins | Serves 2
Ingredients:
  • 300g cooked rice (cold)
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • 100g peas
  • 2 spring onions
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
Method:

Scramble eggs, set aside. Stir-fry rice, add peas. Add eggs, spring onions, soy sauce.

Thu

Quesadilla

10 mins | Serves 1
Ingredients:
  • 2 tortillas
  • 75g cheddar, grated
  • Sliced peppers
  • Salsa, sour cream
Method:

Layer cheese and peppers between tortillas. Dry fry until golden both sides. Serve with salsa and sour cream.

Avocado on toast

Toast bread. Mash avocado with lemon, salt. Spread on toast, sprinkle chilli flakes.

Greek salad

10 mins | Serves 2
Ingredients:

Caesar salad

15 mins | Serves 2

Dinners

Mon

Sausage Casserole

45 mins | Serves 4
Ingredients:
  • 8 pork sausages
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 2 peppers, sliced
  • 2 tins tomatoes
  • 1 tin cannellini beans
  • 2 tsp smoked paprika
Method:

Brown sausages. Add onion, peppers, cook 5 mins. Add tomatoes, paprika, beans, sausages. Cover, simmer 30 mins.

Tue

Tuna Pasta Bake

35 mins | Serves 4
Ingredients:
  • 300g pasta
  • 2 tins tuna
  • 1 tin sweetcorn
  • 50g butter
  • 50g flour
  • 500ml milk
  • 150g cheddar
Method:

Cook pasta. Make cheese sauce with butter, flour, milk, half cheese. Mix pasta, tuna, sweetcorn, sauce. Top with cheese. Bake 200C 20 mins.

Wed

Macaroni Cheese

30 mins | Serves 4
Ingredients:
  • 350g macaroni
  • 50g butter
  • 50g flour
  • 600ml milk
  • 250g cheddar
  • 1 tsp mustard
Method:

Cook macaroni. Make sauce with butter, flour, milk, most of cheese and mustard. Mix with pasta. Top with remaining cheese. Grill until golden.

Thu

Pork Chops with Apple

25 mins | Serves 4
Ingredients:
  • 4 pork chops
  • 2 apples, sliced
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 200ml cider
  • 1 tbsp wholegrain mustard
  • Mashed potato
Method:

Season and fry chops 5 mins each side. Remove. Fry onion, apple. Add cider, mustard, simmer 5 mins. Return chops. Serve with mash.

Fri

Smoked Haddock Risotto

35 mins | Serves 4
Ingredients:
  • 400g smoked haddock
  • 300g risotto rice
  • 1 onion
  • 150ml white wine
  • 1L fish stock
  • 100g peas
  • 50g parmesan
Method:

Poach haddock, flake. Cook onion, add rice. Add wine, then stock ladle by ladle. Add peas and haddock last 5 mins. Stir in parmesan.

Sat

Pulled Pork

4 hours | Serves 6
Ingredients:
  • 1.5kg pork shoulder
  • 2 tbsp smoked paprika
  • 1 tbsp brown sugar
  • 200ml BBQ sauce
  • Burger buns
  • Coleslaw
Method:

Rub pork with paprika, sugar, salt. Slow cook at 150C 3-4 hours until falling apart. Shred, mix with BBQ sauce. Serve in buns with coleslaw.

Sun

Vegetable Stir-fry

15 mins | Serves 4
Ingredients:
  • 1 broccoli, florets
  • 2 peppers, sliced
  • 2 pak choi
  • 100g beansprouts
  • 3 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp sesame oil
  • Noodles or rice
Method:

Stir-fry broccoli 2 mins. Add peppers, pak choi, beansprouts, cook 3 mins. Add soy sauce, sesame oil. Serve with noodles or rice.
