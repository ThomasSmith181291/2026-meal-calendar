Weekend Breakfast

Avocado Toast with Eggs

10 mins | Serves 2
Ingredients:
  • 2 ripe avocados
  • 4 slices sourdough
  • 4 eggs
  • Chilli flakes
  • Lemon juice
  • Salt & pepper
Method:

Toast bread. Mash avocados with lemon, salt, pepper. Poach eggs. Spread avocado on toast, top with poached eggs, sprinkle chilli flakes.

Lunches

Mon

Tomato soup

25 mins | Serves 4
Ingredients:
  • 2 tins chopped tomatoes
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 500ml veg stock
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • Fresh basil
Method:

Soften onion and garlic in oil. Add tomatoes, stock, sugar. Simmer 15 mins. Blend smooth, season, serve with basil and crusty bread.

Tue

Egg fried rice

15 mins | Serves 2
Ingredients:
  • 300g cooked rice (cold)
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • 100g peas
  • 2 spring onions, sliced
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 2 tbsp oil
Method:

Heat oil in wok, scramble eggs, set aside. Stir-fry rice 2 mins, add peas, cook 2 mins. Add eggs, spring onions, soy sauce. Toss together.

Dinners

Mon

Spaghetti Bolognese

45 mins | Serves 4
Ingredients:
  • 500g beef mince
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 2 carrots, diced
  • 2 tins chopped tomatoes
  • 2 tbsp tomato puree
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 400g spaghetti
  • Parmesan to serve
Method:

Brown mince in large pan, drain fat. Add onion, garlic, carrots, cook 5 mins. Add tomatoes, puree, oregano. Simmer 30 mins. Cook spaghetti, drain, serve with sauce and parmesan.

Tue

Chicken Katsu Curry

35 mins | Serves 4
Ingredients:
  • 4 chicken breasts
  • 100g flour
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • 150g panko breadcrumbs
  • For sauce: 1 onion, 2 tbsp curry powder, 1 tbsp flour, 400ml chicken stock, 1 tbsp honey
  • Rice to serve
Method:

Make sauce: fry onion, add curry powder and flour, add stock, simmer 10 mins, blend. Coat chicken in flour, egg, panko. Shallow fry 5 mins each side. Slice, serve with rice and sauce.

Wed

Cauliflower Cheese

35 mins | Serves 4
Ingredients:
  • 1 large cauliflower
  • 50g butter
  • 50g flour
  • 500ml milk
  • 200g mature cheddar, grated
  • 1 tsp mustard
Method:

Break cauliflower into florets, boil 5 mins until just tender. Make sauce: melt butter, add flour, gradually add milk stirring. Add most of cheese and mustard. Put cauliflower in dish, pour over sauce, top with remaining cheese. Bake 200C 20 mins.

Thu

Chicken Fajitas

25 mins | Serves 4
Ingredients:
  • 500g chicken breast, sliced
  • 2 peppers, sliced
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 2 tbsp fajita seasoning
  • 8 tortillas
  • Sour cream, guacamole, salsa to serve
Method:

Toss chicken in seasoning. Fry chicken 5 mins until cooked. Add peppers and onion, cook 5 mins. Warm tortillas. Serve chicken in tortillas with toppings.

Fri

Beef Stew

2 hours | Serves 4
Ingredients:
  • 500g stewing beef, cubed
  • 2 onions, quartered
  • 4 carrots, chunked
  • 4 potatoes, chunked
  • 500ml beef stock
  • 2 tbsp flour
  • 2 tbsp tomato puree
  • Fresh thyme
Method:

Toss beef in flour, brown in batches. Add onions, cook 3 mins. Add all ingredients to pot, bring to simmer. Cover, cook gently 1.5-2 hours until beef is tender. Season well.

Sat

Lamb Koftas

25 mins | Serves 4
Ingredients:
  • 500g lamb mince
  • 1 onion, grated
  • 2 garlic cloves, crushed
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 1 tsp coriander
  • Fresh mint
  • Pitta bread
  • Tzatziki, salad to serve
Method:

Mix lamb, onion, garlic, spices, mint. Shape into sausages around skewers. Grill or BBQ 10-12 mins turning. Serve in pitta with tzatziki and salad.

Sun

Lasagne

1 hour 15 mins | Serves 6
Ingredients:
  • 500g beef mince
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 tins chopped tomatoes
  • Lasagne sheets
  • 50g butter
  • 50g flour
  • 500ml milk
  • 100g parmesan
  • 100g mozzarella
Method:

Make meat sauce: brown mince, add onion, tomatoes, simmer 20 mins. Make white sauce: melt butter, add flour, gradually add milk, stir until thick. Layer: meat, pasta, white sauce. Repeat. Top with cheese. Bake 180C 40 mins.
