Weekend Breakfast

Full English

20 mins | Serves 2
Ingredients:
  • 4 rashers bacon
  • 4 sausages
  • 4 eggs
  • 2 tomatoes, halved
  • 200g mushrooms
  • 1 tin baked beans
  • Toast
Method:

Grill sausages 15 mins turning. Add bacon last 5 mins. Fry mushrooms and tomatoes. Fry or poach eggs. Heat beans. Serve all together with toast.

Lunches

Mon

Carrot & coriander soup

30 mins | Serves 4
Ingredients:
  • 500g carrots, chopped
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1L veg stock
  • 1 tsp ground coriander
  • Fresh coriander
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
Method:

Soften onion in oil 5 mins. Add carrots, ground coriander, stock. Simmer 20 mins until carrots soft. Blend smooth, serve with fresh coriander.

Tue

Cheese toastie

5 mins | Serves 1
Ingredients:
  • 2 slices bread
  • 75g cheddar, grated
  • Butter
Method:

Butter bread on outside. Fill with cheese. Fry in pan until golden both sides and cheese melted.

Wed

Prawn mayo wrap

5 mins | Serves 1
Ingredients:
  • 100g cooked prawns
  • 2 tbsp mayo
  • Squeeze lemon juice
  • 1 wrap
  • Lettuce, cucumber
Method:

Mix prawns with mayo and lemon. Lay wrap flat, add lettuce, cucumber, prawn mixture. Roll up tightly.

Thu

Cheese omelette

10 mins | Serves 1
Ingredients:
  • 3 eggs
  • 50g cheese, grated
  • 1 tbsp butter
  • Salt & pepper
Method:

Beat eggs, season. Melt butter in pan over medium heat. Pour in eggs, swirl to cover base. When nearly set, add cheese to one half, fold over. Serve immediately.

Fri

Tuna melt

10 mins | Serves 1
Ingredients:
  • 1 tin tuna, drained
  • 2 tbsp mayo
  • 2 slices bread
  • 50g cheddar, sliced
Method:

Mix tuna with mayo. Toast bread lightly, spread tuna mixture on top, add cheese. Grill until cheese melted and bubbling.

Dinners

Mon

Chilli Con Carne

45 mins | Serves 4
Ingredients:
  • 500g beef mince
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 1 tin chopped tomatoes
  • 1 tin kidney beans
  • 2 tsp cumin
  • 1 tsp chilli powder
  • Rice, sour cream to serve
Method:

Brown mince, add onion, garlic. Add spices, cook 1 min. Add tomatoes, beans. Simmer 30 mins. Serve with rice, sour cream, grated cheese.

Tue

Chicken Leek Pie

1 hour | Serves 4
Ingredients:
  • 500g chicken breast, cubed
  • 3 leeks, sliced
  • 200ml chicken stock
  • 200ml double cream
  • 1 tbsp Dijon mustard
  • 1 sheet puff pastry
  • 1 egg, beaten
Method:

Poach chicken in stock 15 mins, remove. Cook leeks in stock until soft. Add cream, mustard, return chicken. Pour into pie dish. Top with pastry, brush with egg. Bake 200C 25 mins until golden.

Wed

Mushroom Risotto

35 mins | Serves 4
Ingredients:
  • 300g risotto rice
  • 300g mixed mushrooms, sliced
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 150ml white wine
  • 1L hot veg stock
  • 50g parmesan
  • 2 tbsp butter
Method:

Fry mushrooms in butter, set aside. Cook onion and garlic, add rice, stir 1 min. Add wine, stir until absorbed. Add stock ladle by ladle, stirring, until rice is creamy (20 mins). Stir in mushrooms, parmesan, butter.

Thu

Beef Stroganoff

25 mins | Serves 4
Ingredients:
  • 500g beef sirloin, sliced thin
  • 250g mushrooms, sliced
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 200ml beef stock
  • 150ml sour cream
  • 1 tbsp paprika
  • Rice or pasta to serve
Method:

Fry beef quickly over high heat, set aside. Cook onion, garlic, mushrooms 5 mins. Add paprika, stock, simmer 5 mins. Return beef, stir in sour cream. Serve with rice or pasta.

Fri

Fish Tacos

25 mins | Serves 4
Ingredients:
  • 400g white fish fillets
  • 2 tsp cumin
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 8 small tortillas
  • 1/4 cabbage, shredded
  • Lime
  • Sour cream
  • Fresh coriander
Method:

Season fish with cumin, paprika, salt. Pan fry or grill 3-4 mins each side. Flake fish. Warm tortillas. Fill with cabbage, fish, sour cream, coriander, lime juice.

Sat

Lamb Koftas

25 mins | Serves 4
Ingredients:
  • 500g lamb mince
  • 1 onion, grated
  • 2 garlic cloves, crushed
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 1 tsp coriander
  • Fresh mint
  • Pitta bread
  • Tzatziki, salad to serve
Method:

Mix lamb, onion, garlic, spices, mint. Shape into sausages around skewers. Grill or BBQ 10-12 mins turning. Serve in pitta with tzatziki and salad.

Sun

Chicken Tikka Masala

40 mins | Serves 4
Ingredients:
  • 500g chicken breast, cubed
  • 2 tbsp tikka paste
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 1 tin chopped tomatoes
  • 200ml double cream
  • Fresh coriander
  • Rice to serve
Method:

Coat chicken in half the tikka paste. Fry chicken until golden, set aside. Cook onion and garlic, add remaining paste, tomatoes. Simmer 10 mins, add cream and chicken. Cook 10 mins. Serve with rice and coriander.
