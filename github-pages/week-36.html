Week 36

31 Aug - 06 Sep 2026

Weekend Breakfast

American Pancakes

20 mins | Serves 4
Ingredients:
  • 200g self-raising flour
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1 egg
  • 250ml milk
  • 2 tbsp melted butter
  • Maple syrup, blueberries
Method:

Mix dry ingredients. Add egg, milk, butter. Cook small rounds until bubbles form, flip. Stack and serve with syrup and berries.

Lunches

Mon

Cheese on toast

5 mins | Serves 1
Ingredients:
  • 2 slices bread
  • 75g cheddar, grated
  • Worcestershire sauce
Method:

Toast bread one side. Flip, cover with cheese, dash of Worcestershire. Grill until bubbling.

Tue

Sardines on toast

5 mins | Serves 1
Ingredients:
  • 1 tin sardines
  • 2 slices bread
  • Butter
  • Lemon
  • Black pepper
Method:

Toast and butter bread. Top with sardines, lemon juice, pepper.

Wed

Egg fried rice

15 mins | Serves 2
Ingredients:
  • 300g cooked rice (cold)
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • 100g peas
  • 2 spring onions
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
Method:

Scramble eggs, set aside. Stir-fry rice, add peas. Add eggs, spring onions, soy sauce.

Thu

Quesadilla

10 mins | Serves 1
Ingredients:
  • 2 tortillas
  • 75g cheddar, grated
  • Sliced peppers
  • Salsa, sour cream
Method:

Layer cheese and peppers between tortillas. Dry fry until golden both sides. Serve with salsa and sour cream.

Fri

Avocado on toast

5 mins | Serves 1
Ingredients:
  • 1 avocado
  • 2 slices sourdough
  • Lemon juice
  • Chilli flakes
  • Salt
Method:

Toast bread. Mash avocado with lemon, salt. Spread on toast, sprinkle chilli flakes.

Caesar salad

15 mins | Serves 2
Ingredients:

Dinners

Mon

Thai Green Curry

25 mins | Serves 4
Ingredients:
  • 500g chicken thigh, sliced
  • 2 tbsp green curry paste
  • 400ml coconut milk
  • 1 aubergine, cubed
  • Green beans
  • Thai basil
  • Rice
Method:

Fry paste 1 min. Add chicken, cook 3 mins. Add coconut milk, aubergine. Simmer 15 mins. Add beans last 5 mins. Serve with rice.

Tue

Tuna Pasta Bake

35 mins | Serves 4
Ingredients:
  • 300g pasta
  • 2 tins tuna
  • 1 tin sweetcorn
  • 50g butter
  • 50g flour
  • 500ml milk
  • 150g cheddar
Method:

Cook pasta. Make cheese sauce with butter, flour, milk, half cheese. Mix pasta, tuna, sweetcorn, sauce. Top with cheese. Bake 200C 20 mins.

Wed

Mushroom Risotto

35 mins | Serves 4
Ingredients:
  • 300g risotto rice
  • 300g mushrooms
  • 1 onion
  • 150ml white wine
  • 1L veg stock
  • 50g parmesan
  • Fresh thyme
Method:

Fry mushrooms, set aside. Cook onion, add rice. Add wine, then stock ladle by ladle, stirring. Add mushrooms, parmesan, thyme.

Thu

Chicken Stir-fry

20 mins | Serves 4
Ingredients:
  • 500g chicken breast, sliced
  • 2 peppers, sliced
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 2 pak choi
  • 3 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp honey
  • Noodles
Method:

Stir-fry chicken 5 mins. Add vegetables, cook 3 mins. Add soy sauce, honey. Serve with noodles.

Fri

Smoked Haddock Risotto

35 mins | Serves 4
Ingredients:
  • 400g smoked haddock
  • 300g risotto rice
  • 1 onion
  • 150ml white wine
  • 1L fish stock
  • 100g peas
  • 50g parmesan
Method:

Poach haddock, flake. Cook onion, add rice. Add wine, then stock ladle by ladle. Add peas and haddock last 5 mins. Stir in parmesan.

Sat

Chicken Pie

1 hour | Serves 4
Ingredients:
  • 500g chicken breast, cubed
  • 3 leeks, sliced
  • 200ml chicken stock
  • 200ml cream
  • 1 tbsp Dijon mustard
  • 1 sheet puff pastry
  • 1 egg
Method:

Poach chicken in stock. Cook leeks in stock. Add cream, mustard, chicken. Pour into dish. Top with pastry, brush with egg. Bake 200C 25 mins.

Sun

Vegetable Curry

30 mins | Serves 4
Ingredients:
  • 1 cauliflower, florets
  • 2 potatoes, cubed
  • 200g spinach
  • 1 onion
  • 3 tbsp curry paste
  • 400ml coconut milk
  • Rice
Method:

Fry onion, add paste. Add cauliflower, potatoes, coconut milk. Simmer 20 mins. Stir in spinach. Serve with rice.
