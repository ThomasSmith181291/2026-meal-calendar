Weekend Breakfast

American Pancakes

20 mins | Serves 4
Ingredients:
  • 200g self-raising flour
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1 egg
  • 250ml milk
  • 2 tbsp melted butter
  • Pinch salt
  • Maple syrup, blueberries, bacon
Method:

Mix dry ingredients. Add egg, milk, butter, whisk until smooth. Cook small rounds in buttered pan until bubbles form, flip. Stack and serve with toppings.

Lunches

Mon

Butternut squash soup

40 mins | Serves 4
Ingredients:
  • 1 butternut squash, peeled and cubed
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 1L veg stock
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
Method:

Roast squash with oil at 200C for 25 mins. Soften onion and garlic. Add squash, cumin, stock. Simmer 10 mins, blend smooth.

Wed

Croque monsieur

15 mins | Serves 2
Ingredients:
  • 4 slices bread
  • 4 slices ham
  • 100g gruyere, grated
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1 tbsp flour
  • 150ml milk
  • 1 tsp Dijon mustard
Method:

Make sauce: melt 1 tbsp butter, add flour, stir in milk until thick, add half cheese. Layer bread, ham, sauce. Top with remaining cheese. Bake 200C 10 mins until golden.

Dinners

Mon

Cottage Pie

1 hour | Serves 4
Ingredients:
  • 500g beef mince
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 carrots, diced
  • 400ml beef stock
  • 2 tbsp tomato puree
  • 1 tsp Worcestershire sauce
  • 800g potatoes
  • 50g butter
  • 50ml milk
Method:

Brown mince, add onion, carrots. Add stock, puree, Worcestershire, simmer 20 mins. Boil potatoes, mash with butter and milk. Put mince in dish, top with mash. Bake 200C 25 mins until golden.

Tue

Thai Green Curry

25 mins | Serves 4
Ingredients:
  • 500g chicken thigh, sliced
  • 2 tbsp green curry paste
  • 400ml coconut milk
  • 1 aubergine, cubed
  • 100g green beans
  • Thai basil
  • Fish sauce
  • Rice to serve
Method:

Fry curry paste 1 min. Add chicken, cook 3 mins. Pour in coconut milk, add aubergine. Simmer 15 mins. Add beans last 5 mins. Season with fish sauce, serve with rice and basil.

Wed

Lasagne

1 hour 15 mins | Serves 6
Ingredients:
  • 500g beef mince
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 tins chopped tomatoes
  • Lasagne sheets
  • 50g butter
  • 50g flour
  • 500ml milk
  • 100g parmesan
  • 100g mozzarella
Method:

Make meat sauce: brown mince, add onion, tomatoes, simmer 20 mins. Make white sauce: melt butter, add flour, gradually add milk, stir until thick. Layer: meat, pasta, white sauce. Repeat. Top with cheese. Bake 180C 40 mins.

Thu

Sausage Casserole

45 mins | Serves 4
Ingredients:
  • 8 pork sausages
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 2 peppers, sliced
  • 2 tins chopped tomatoes
  • 1 tin cannellini beans
  • 2 tsp smoked paprika
  • Crusty bread to serve
Method:

Brown sausages in pan, set aside. Cook onion and peppers 5 mins. Add tomatoes, paprika, beans, return sausages. Cover, simmer 30 mins. Serve with crusty bread.

Fri

Fish & Chips

45 mins | Serves 4
Ingredients:
  • 4 cod fillets
  • 150g plain flour
  • 200ml cold sparkling water
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1kg potatoes, cut into chips
  • Oil for frying
  • Mushy peas, tartare sauce
Method:

Soak chips in water 30 mins, dry well. Fry chips at 160C 5 mins, then 190C until golden. Mix flour, baking powder, water for batter. Dip fish in batter, fry at 180C 6-8 mins. Serve with chips, peas, tartare.

Sunday Roast (for 7)

Roast Pork

2.5 hours | Serves 6-8
Ingredients:
  • 2kg pork shoulder or leg, skin scored
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 tbsp sea salt
  • Fresh sage
Method:

Dry skin thoroughly. Rub with oil and salt, getting into scores. Roast at 220C 30 mins for crackling. Reduce to 160C, cook 2 hours. Rest 20 mins. Crisp crackling under grill if needed.

Sides

Roast Potatoes

1 hour | Serves 6
Ingredients:
  • 1.5kg potatoes, peeled and quartered
  • 4 tbsp goose fat or olive oil
  • Salt
  • Fresh rosemary (optional)
Method:

Boil potatoes 10 mins, drain. Shake in pan to roughen edges. Heat fat in roasting tin at 200C. Add potatoes, turn to coat. Roast 45-50 mins, turning halfway, until golden and crispy.

Yorkshire Puddings

30 mins | Serves 12
Ingredients:
  • 140g plain flour
  • 4 eggs
  • 200ml milk
  • Sunflower oil
Method:

Mix flour, eggs, milk until smooth. Rest 30 mins. Put 1 tsp oil in each hole of 12-hole muffin tin. Heat at 230C until smoking. Quickly pour in batter. Bake 20-25 mins until risen and golden. Don't open oven door!

Onion Gravy

25 mins | Serves 6
Ingredients:
  • 2 onions, sliced
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1 tbsp flour
  • 500ml beef stock
  • Splash of red wine (optional)
  • Meat juices from roasting tin
Method:

Cook onions in butter over low heat 15 mins until soft and golden. Add flour, stir 1 min. Add wine if using, then stock and meat juices. Simmer 5 mins, season.
