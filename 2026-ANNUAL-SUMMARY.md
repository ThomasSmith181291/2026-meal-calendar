## SEASONAL HIGHLIGHTS

### Winter (Jan-Mar)
- Root vegetables: parsnips, carrots, swede
- Brassicas: cabbage, Brussels sprouts, kale
- Comfort food: stews, pies, casseroles

### Spring (Apr-Jun)
- Asparagus (peak April-June)
- New potatoes, broad beans, peas
- Fresh greens: spinach, watercress
- Lighter dishes, first BBQs

### Summer (Jul-Sep)
- Tomatoes, courgettes, peppers
- Berries: strawberries, raspberries
- Sweetcorn, runner beans
- Salads, BBQ, outdoor eating

### Autumn (Oct-Dec)
- Squash, pumpkin
- Apples, pears, plums
- Wild mushrooms
- Back to comfort food

## TODDLER NOTES

All meals can be adapted for your 3-year-old:
- **Spicy dishes:** Set aside portion before adding heat
- **Curries:** Make mild version
- **Stir-fries:** Less soy sauce, no chilli
- **Fish:** Remove bones, small pieces
- **Avoid:** Olives (as noted)

**Toddler favourites included:**
- Fish fingers
- Pasta with mild sauce
- Bangers & mash
- Chicken pieces
- Rice dishes
- Pizza
- Roast dinners (small portions)
